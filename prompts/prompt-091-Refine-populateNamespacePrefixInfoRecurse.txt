Further refine the populateNamespacePrefixInfoRecurse method:
- After determining the values for the NamespacePrefixInfo object, but before calling it recursively for child XElement objects, look at the attributes for the current XElement object. In this case, generate a p# prefix for that namespace, and add it to the namespacePrefixPairs array.
- When generating a p# prefix, replace the # with the value in pHashCount, and then increment pHashCount.
